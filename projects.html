Smartseeker Drone Final Year Project


For our Final Year Project we got a chance to work with a DJI Tello drone. Our goal is to make it so that it can help you find certain items you are looking for. It incorporates AI as the internal logic to help the drone figure out the room layout and where the objects is possibly located.
For example, a TV which is in the living room, if the AI sees a couch, it gives information back to the drone to look at the wall opposite of the couch to find the TV since in most households that is where the TV is located. In conclussion the AI is mostly in control of the drone and make the drones decision for it making it easier for the user seeking any specific item.
